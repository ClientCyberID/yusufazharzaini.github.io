  PRODUCTS

BEARING

Bearings are our origin. High quality bearings are required to make safe cars. Our bearings have the highest performing quality, therefore our bearings are used in many parts of various cars. Also our bearings are using in various fields other than automotive.

HUB

We can produce 8 kinds of various Hubs with 1000t to 2800t presses. Improvement of billets cutting accuracy brought precision closed forging and improved steel bar yield.

CONSTANT VELOCITY JOINT

We are designing and manufacturing mold dies for Constant velocity joints that transfer engines power to wheels in house. Hot forging and warm forging improved surface roughness and dimention accuracy during the forging process. It reduced over 30% of electrical usage, also it can help CO2 reduction.

TRANSMISSION GEAR

Transmission gears are one of the parts which requir high accuracy in essential automobile parts. These parts are in production with combined ring rolling and press forging which gathered our technologies. This combined forging line improved product accuracy, hardness and steel bar yield. From the result of this improvement, we are receiving high evaluation from the automotive industry.
